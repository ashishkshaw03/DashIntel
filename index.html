How to Pitch in Rajdhanwar?

1

The "Local" Connection

Dhanwar ke log bharose par business karte hain. Baat shuru karte waqt kisi purane client ya school ka reference dein (e.g. Elite School). "Main Dhanwar se hi hoon" kehne se ice break hota hai.

2

Fear of Loss (FOMO)

Unhe batayein ki Giridih ya Hazaribagh ke schools digital ho chuke hain, aur Dhanwar ke parents ab digital reports mangte hain. Agar wo piche rahe toh admissions kam ho jayenge.

3

Visual Demo

Features mat ginvaiye, demo dikhaiye. Mobile app mein unka khud ka logo dikha kar bole: "Sir, kal se parents aapka school apne phone par dekhenge."

4

The Close

Rajdhanwar mein 'Cash' aur 'Personal Support' chalta hai. Bolen: "Main yahi se hoon, koi bhi dikat hogi toh 1 ghante mein office pahunch jaunga."
